Features

Everything you need to ace it

Built for developers and professionals who want an unfair advantage in technical interviews.

Real-time Transcription
Deepgram Nova-3 captures every word with <300ms latency. Interim and final results stream instantly to your screen.
AI Answer Generation
Gemini 2.5 Flash crafts a confident, concise English answer in real time — streamed word by word so you can read ahead.
6 Language Translations
Sinhala, Tamil, Chinese, French, Spanish, German. Switch instantly — the AI prompt updates automatically.
Tab Audio Capture
Works with Zoom, Meet, Teams. Share your browser tab and Copilot captures the interviewer audio — no plugins needed.
Smart Silence Detection
VAD events and sentence-end detection trigger AI the instant a question finishes — no lag, no duplicate processing.
Private by Design
Audio never touches a third-party storage layer. All processing is per-session and discarded on disconnect.
How it works

Three steps to interview confidence

No setup headaches. Open the app, share your tab, and let the AI do the heavy lifting.

01
Choose your language & launch
Pick Sinhala, Tamil, Chinese, or any supported language from the switcher. Click Launch Copilot to open the live session.
02
Share your browser tab audio
Click Start — Share Tab Audio. In the browser dialog, select the video call tab and tick "Share Tab Audio". The session goes live instantly.
03
Read your answer as it streams in
The moment the interviewer finishes a question, the AI translates it and streams a confident English answer word-by-word. Read it, say it, ace the question.
